About us

This is our rescue team we, started off as a group of friends who all wanted to give a better life to these wonderful animals.

Hi, my name is Olivia. I've been a member of Onlydogs for a few years. We found these two guys on the street in Ireland starving on the side of the road. Now they have all the food they could have dreamed of.

Hi, my name is Charlotte. I've been a member of Onlydogs since it founded. We have rescued hundreds dogs and given them a forever home. The best part with OnlyDogs is to see the difference from when we first meet the dogs until they go to their new families. All the dogs I have had the honor of meeting have been wonderful.

Hi, my name is Ryan. I've been a member of Onlydogs for just a few months. This is my best buddy here, I call him Pancake because he always begins to wave his tail when I talk about pancakes. I really love being a part of OnlyDogs and getting to help these sweet animals.
