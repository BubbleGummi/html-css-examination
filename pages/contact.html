Contact us











THANK YOU FOR YOUR INTEREST IN ONLYDOGS!

All visits require an approved application and an appointment.

If you want to meet dogs but do not have a specific dog in mind, please describe what you’re looking for and we will follow up with dogs that fits your needs and lifestyle, if we have a match available.

We love donations!

If you have dog-related supplies you’d like to donate, we would be grateful to accept them! We welcome dog food and treats, beds, crates, gently used toys, apparel, blankets and more. You can drop them off at the Refuge anytime between 7am and 8pm Monday through Sunday; if no one is there, you can leave them outside and someone will be back shortly to bring them inside. Thank you in advance for your generosity!
